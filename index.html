Here are some of my personal projects created during my studies at the University Paris 7 Diderot

Priority Inversion and Inheritance

The purpose of this little project was to create a priority inversion between 3 threads and detect it. Then, with priority inheritance, successfully prevent this inversion and detect it also.

Spiral Matrix Filling

The project goal was to fill a spiral matrix, the size of the matrix is ​​entered by the user.

measure of creation, termination and context change

The purpose of this project was to measure the time of creation and termination of forks and threads. And finally to measure the time of a context change with pipes and semaphores between two forks and two threads.
